Voice & Audio Translate

Voice & Audio Translate app lets you translate speech (online) and audio files (offline) into several languages. You could record your own audio files and upload them for translation. You could also import audio files from your cloud storage such as iCloud, Dropbox, Google Drive, iTunes media library etc. Stores upto 5 of your previously translated files which you could delete right from the app. The app provides authenticated signup & login mechanism or you could use your existing Google signin or Apple signin to use the app features completely free of charge.
